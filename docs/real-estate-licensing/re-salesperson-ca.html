Exam Details

Question TypesMultiple Choice
FormatLinear; national and state portions (most states); online proctored or test center
Online ProctoringAvailable
ID RequirementsGovernment-issued photo ID required. Name on ID must match registration exactly. Some states require two forms of ID.
RenewalRequired -- Real estate licenses are typically valid for 2 years. Renewal requires continuing education (CE) hours, which vary by state (e.g., CA requires 45 CE hours, TX requires 18 CE hours). Renewal deadlines and fees are set by each state's real estate commission.
PrerequisitesAge 18+; must complete 3 college-level real estate courses before applying (recommended)
Retake PolicyRetake policies vary by state. Most states allow unlimited retakes with a waiting period (typically 24 hours to 30 days). Some states charge the full exam fee for each attempt. CA allows unlimited retakes; TX requires waiting 24 hours after failure.
